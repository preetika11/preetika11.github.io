Teaching

As a teaching assistant for the geospatial technologies course in the Department of Biosystems Engineering at Auburn University, I assisted students with ArcGIS-related labs, graded their assignments and demonstrated the use of ArcPy. I taught them how to write ArcPy code for their lab assignments, which automated the process of importing shapefiles, creating feature layers, performing various analyses and creating maps within the working environment.
